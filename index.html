About Studio Nela

We're a design studio that believes in the power of simplicity and purposeful creation.

Founded in 2020, Studio Nela is a boutique design agency specializing in creating elegant, minimal digital experiences for forward-thinking brands. We combine strategic thinking with exceptional craft to build products that stand the test of time.

Our team of designers, strategists, and developers work closely with ambitious founders and teams to transform complex ideas into clear, beautiful solutions. Every project is an opportunity to push boundaries while maintaining a calm, focused aesthetic.

We've partnered with startups, Fortune 500 companies, and everything in between — helping them establish their visual identity, design digital products, and create experiences that resonate with their audiences.

Our Values

  • Clarity over complexity — We believe great design simplifies.
  • Craft matters — Every pixel, every word, every interaction counts.
  • Long-term thinking — We build systems that scale and endure.
  • Collaborative spirit — The best work happens when we work together.

Services

From strategy to systems, we design with precision and purpose.

Brand Identity

Naming, logotypes, typography, color systems, and comprehensive brand guidelines that scale with your business and maintain consistency across all touchpoints.

Product Design

User research, experience architecture, interface design, prototyping, and design systems for web and mobile applications that users love.

Websites

Marketing sites, landing pages, and corporate websites built for speed, clarity, and conversion. Custom CMS integration and responsive design included.

Art Direction

Photography direction, motion design, illustration, and visual language development that elevates your brand and creates memorable experiences.

Strategy & Consulting

Brand positioning, competitive analysis, user research, and design audits to ensure your digital presence aligns with business goals.

Development

Front-end development, animation implementation, performance optimization, and technical partnerships to bring designs to life with precision.

Our Process

A structured approach that delivers exceptional results.

01. Discovery

We start by understanding your goals, audience, and competitive landscape through research and stakeholder interviews.

02. Strategy

Define positioning, create user personas, map journeys, and establish a clear creative direction.

03. Design

Craft high-fidelity designs, interactive prototypes, and comprehensive design systems with iterative feedback.

04. Delivery

Hand off polished assets, collaborate with developers, and ensure the final product matches our vision.
